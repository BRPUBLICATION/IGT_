About Our Association

The Artificial Intelligence & Security Association is a non-profit organization founded to promote the development and application of AI in security.

Our members come from diverse backgrounds including academia, industry, and government, united by a common interest in advancing AI applications for security challenges. We believe AI has the potential to revolutionize security practices while maintaining ethical standards.

Our Mission

  • Advance AI development for security applications
  • Promote collaboration across industry and academia
  • Establish ethical guidelines for AI in security
  • Support innovation and entrepreneurship in the field

Key Activities

  • Annual Global AI Security Summit
  • Quarterly research publications
  • Professional certification programs
  • Industry working groups
  • Startup incubation program

Membership Benefits

Join our global network of AI and security professionals to access exclusive resources, networking opportunities, and professional development programs.

Fellow

$199 / year
  • All Professional benefits
  • 20% discount on conference tickets
  • Full research library access
  • Invitation to member-only events
  • Eligibility for committees
  • Mentorship program access

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Bachelor’s degree or higher in CS, Cybersecurity, AI, or related field
  • Minimum 5 years of professional experience in AI/Cybersecurity
  • 10+ articles published in major international journals
  • Participation in AI/Cybersecurity-related conferences or forums
  • Active involvement in industry/academic projects
  • Endorsement by a current AISA member or recommendation letter
Join Now

Professional Fellow

$499 / year
  • Access to member-only webinars
  • 10% discount on conference tickets
  • Monthly newsletter
  • Listing in member directory
  • Basic research library access

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Master’s degree or professional certifications (CISSP, CEH, etc.)
  • Minimum 7 years of experience in AI/Cybersecurity
  • Leadership or major contributions in projects or research
  • 15+ peer-reviewed publications or notable industry outputs
  • Judged or served as panelist at conferences (IEEE, Springer, ICM)
  • Endorsed by two Professional or Distinguished Fellows

Distinguished Fellow

By Nominations / year
  • All Fellow benefits
  • 30% discount on conference tickets
  • Free access to training programs
  • Speaking opportunities at events
  • Advisory roles
  • Executive networking

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Ph.D. or equivalent expertise in AI, ML, Big Data, or Cybersecurity
  • 10+ years of distinguished service in industry, academia, or policy
  • Led multi-institutional collaborations or won major awards
  • Served on boards, editorial panels, or steering committees
  • Track record in mentorship and community engagement
  • Endorsed by three Distinguished Fellows & AISA Executive approval
